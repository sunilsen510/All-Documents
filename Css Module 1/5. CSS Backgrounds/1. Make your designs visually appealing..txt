ЁЯТм 6. Interview Questions and Answers
ЁЯЯв Basic

Q1. What makes a website visually appealing?
A: Proper color harmony, spacing, readable typography, balanced layout, and consistency make a website visually appealing.

Q2. Why is white space important in design?
A: It improves readability, focus, and prevents cluttered layouts.

Q3. How many colors should you use in a design?
A: Ideally 3 main colors тАФ Primary, Secondary, and Neutral тАФ to maintain balance.

Q4. What is visual hierarchy?
A: The arrangement of elements that guides the viewerтАЩs eyes in order of importance (e.g., heading тЖТ subheading тЖТ content тЖТ button).

ЁЯЯб Intermediate

Q5. How does color psychology affect design?
A: Different colors evoke different emotions тАФ blue (trust), red (energy), green (growth), yellow (optimism).

Q6. What is the difference between UI and Visual Design?
A: UI focuses on usability (how things work); Visual design focuses on aesthetics (how things look).

Q7. How do typography and font choice impact design?
A: Fonts set the tone тАФ serif fonts feel traditional; sans-serif feels modern and clean.

ЁЯФ╡ Advanced

Q8. How do you maintain consistency in design systems?
A: By using predefined color palettes, typography scales, spacing rules, and reusable UI components.

Q9. WhatтАЩs the role of contrast in accessibility?
A: High contrast ensures readability for visually impaired users тАФ WCAG recommends 4.5:1 ratio for text.

Q10. How can animations improve visual appeal without hurting performance?
A: Use CSS transitions and keyframes sparingly; keep them lightweight (0.2тАУ0.4s) and GPU-friendly (transform, opacity).
